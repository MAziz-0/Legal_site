Financial and Property Matters

In divorce matters, our experience ranges from relatively straightforward cases with limited resources, to those involving complex issues and considerable assets. We will do our best to agree a financial settlement or in the event that agreement is not possible, achieve a realistic and beneficial arrangement through the courts. Should court involvement be required the court has the power to make the following orders: lump sum payments, property adjustment orders to transfer property into the ownership of a husband or wife, or maintenance payments.
